3-DOF Robotic Harvester System

Embedded control • Trajectory planning • Hardware debugging

Built the control stack for a multi-motor manipulator, including trajectory planning, encoder feedback, and closed-loop motor control. When the robot could not achieve the commanded behavior through tuning alone, I used an oscilloscope to compare requested PWM against the actual signal, identified a timing mismatch in the software-timed PWM library, switched libraries, verified the duty cycle, and deployed the corrected controller.
